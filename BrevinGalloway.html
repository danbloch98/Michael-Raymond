BREVIN GALLOWAY

Brevin Galloway got in with the Raymond Representation team as a part of the NIL agreement. Galloway hails from Seneca, South Carolina where he began his high school career at Seneca High School. Coming out of high school, Galloway was rated a three-star prospect and the fourth-ranked player in the state. His high school career was nothing short of spectacular as he tallied three all-state, three all-region, and three all-area first-team selections. Following high school, he went on to play at the College of Charleston where averaged 15 ppg, 3 rpg, and 2.8 apg in his senior season as a starter. Following the 2020-21 season, he entered his name into the NCAA transfer portal and joined the Boston College Eagles for the remainder of his college career.
