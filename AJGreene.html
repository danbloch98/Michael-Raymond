AJ GREENE

Jelani “AJ” Greene is another one of RRs' business and marketing clients. Greene played wide receiver for the University of New Hampshire Wildcats in 2016 and was able to use his success to grow his social media brand. Greene has built a following of around 2 million on his Tik Tok and 150k on Instagram. Throughout his presence on social media, he has seen his content get featured on some of the biggest accounts including ESPN, Bleacher Report, and Sports Illustrated.
