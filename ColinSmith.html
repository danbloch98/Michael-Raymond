COLLIN SMITH

Collin Smith is one of Raymond Representations NIL clients. Smith is from Jacksonville, Florida, and played his high school basketball at The Bolles School. Smith has been proving his doubters wrong from a young age. He came out of high school not highly recruited until he committed to George Washington University. At GW, he played in all 35 games averaging 5.3 ppg and 4.4 rpg. In the 2018-2019 season, Smith took his talents to the University of Central Florida where he played two seasons. At UCF, Smith took his game to a new level starting in all but one game during his two-year stint. He averaged 10.2 points and 5.7 rebounds. Smith has made transferred out of UCF and will play for the North Carolina A&T Aggies.
