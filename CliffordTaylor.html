Clifford Taylor

Clifford Taylor is one of Raymond Representations' business and marketing clients. Taylor was a walk-on tight end at the University of Florida where was able to use his social media to build his brand. Across all of his social media accounts, he has a combined following of over 650k which has allowed him to partner with brands such as Gatorade and Wilson Athletics amongst others with the assistance of the Raymond Representation team.
