ERIC DAVIS

Eric Davis came out of Arthur Hill high school in Saginaw, Michigan with an already impressive resume. In 2013, he was named to both the MaxPreps All-American third team and the Associated Press Boys Basketball Class A All-State first team. Not to mention, he was also a four-star prospect and ranked the number 13 shooting guard in the country and number 2 player in Michigan in 2015 after averaging 25 ppg, 7 rpg, and 6 apg. Davis played his basketball career for the University of Texas Longhorns where he led the team in three-point shooting efficiency during his freshman season at 38.2% and played in every game. Throughout his career for the Longhorns, he played a valuable role in all three seasons having totaled 8.8 ppg, 2.4 rpg, and shot at a 39.7% average.
