COLIN CASTLETON

The Name, Image, and Likeliness agreement brought Colin Castleton to the RR team. Castleton has played a pivotal role throughout his career where he has played. Coming out of Father Lopez Catholic high school in Daytona, Florida, he was rated a four-star prospect averaging 24.5 ppg, 11.7 rpg, and 5.6 blk as a senior. He was voted in the top-10 for Florida’s Mr. Basketball Award and also received FABC 7A Player of the Year honors. The 6’11 forward played his first two college seasons at the University of Michigan where he averaged 2.3 ppg. Castleton then transferred to the University of Florida and has flourished into a staple for the team averaging 12.4 ppg, 6.4 rpg, and 2.3 blk. During his junior season with the Gators, he loaded up on accolades earning SEC Player of the Week two times and All-SEC second-team honors along with many more.
