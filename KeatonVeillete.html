KEATON VEILETTE

Veillette is one of Raymond Reps golf clients and officially joined the team back in June of 2019. He is a professional golfer hailing from Toronto, Ontario, Canada, and comes from a decorated background. Dating back to 2015, Veillette has four first-place finishes to his name and 12 total top-25 finishes as well. In addition to his impressive finishes, he was also named the 2017 Player of the Year.
